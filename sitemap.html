A Group Started in the Year 2010 By Anjali Tikone.Shree Raya Group Believes In creating quality construction of buildings, row house and Bunglows with talented team of experienced technical professional to get the best and fastest Development.

Having Started its Passion of Building homes at Fastest growing city of Panvel, it started with its Projects of Shree Niwas at Vichumbe and Shree Vihar at Aakruli at the Banner of Raya associates.

Shree Raya group took a Leap in his Success Story with the Advent of the New Managing Director Suresh Kamthe, Both Joined to Form a company by the Name of Shree Raya Developers Pvt ltd an ISO 9001:2008 Certified. Its started its Growth story with his ambitious Project of "Raya City" a proposed 25 Acres Township at Koproli and also Row House and Bungalow Project at Aakruli.

Shree Raya Group Believes in Pursuing Various Projects Which will Help Upgrade the lifestyle of New Panvel City and Give new Lease of Life to the lower and middle income Families ,Raya Group Insists Upon Superior Quality Construction Timely Possession and transparent Dealing to achieve this we Hire best in the Business from contractors or may be Architects.

Shree Raya Group consist of three Companies Raya Associates, Hari Om Enterprises And Shree Raya Developers Pvt Ltd..
